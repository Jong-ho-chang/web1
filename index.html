The Wotld Wide Web (abbreviated WWW or the Web) is an information space where documents and other web resources are indentified by Uniform Resource Location (URLs), interlinked by Hypertext links, and accessible via the Internet. [1] English scientist Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989. He wrote the first web brewser in 1990 while employed at CERN in Switzerland.
